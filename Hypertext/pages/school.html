Robert M. Beren Academy is a K-12 grades school. It is a Modern Orthodox private school, and it is where I went for 6-12th grade. I met some of my best friends there and I am eternally grateful for my experiences and the memories that I had while attending school there. Some people were not as fortunate as I was to have such an amazing high school, and middle school, experince. I don't really remember middle school all too well, but I do have a few memories that are probably best not shared because I was a slight bully #ReginaGeorge. High School though is a different story. My graduating class was 17 kids including myself. I know all of the kids, their siblings names, their birthdays, and pretty much thier social security numbers. The entire high school had roughly 66 high schoolers for all 4 years. The number did fluctuate but not enough to make a difference. I also knew everyones names and their life story because everyone was friends. This close knit family and friend group that I had will be an amazing memory I can cherish forever.


Down below are a few fun facts about Beren:

  • The head of the school changed 3 times while I was in High School.
  • Our school was on national TV for our basketball teams drama
  • Three couples are now married, one may be on the way!
  • At least one kid from everyclass has joined the Israeli Army
