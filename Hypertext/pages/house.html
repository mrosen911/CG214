I lived in Houston, Texas for the first 18 years of my life. I still consider it home and plan on moving back in the near future. There is something about the place you grew up that stays with you forever. I never moved houses, I was at the same address for my entire life. Actually, my parents are wanting to move and have started to pack the house, so this is quite sad. I loved my house, it wasn't the biggest or the fanciest, but it was home.
